Original practice bank

These boards were created to exercise familiar names, titles, objects, places, phrases, and multiword patterns. They are not claims about future questions and have not all received an individual source audit.

How the app matches the format

Rounds 1 and 2 use progressive clues. Speed uses a category without clues for 2:30. Ultimate asks for three answers in 60 seconds. The app also records the moment you buzz, rather than charging your typing time against your solving time.
